About AURS

Aerial and Underwater Robotics Society

AURS is the first ever Autonomous Robotics society of Jaypee University of Information Technology. It is a student group working in the field of Autonomous Aerial and Underwater robotics. We are a small team of 8 undergraduates form various branches of Undergraduate Course. Our prime objective is to take part in the annual Robotics Competition organized majorly in our Country.
Society members apply their conceptual understanding and applications in real life implementation of engineering. Our objective includes imparting a better and competitive environment in Robotics and Engineering.
